## Example 2: Cultural Practice Authentication (Medium Confidence)

This example shows cultural practice verification requiring additional community consultation.

```markdown

Content should not be published until critical community consultations complete.

### Verification Confidence Level

**MEDIUM CONFIDENCE (75%)**

Confidence based on: (1) Current practitioner confirmation of techniques, (2) Fishing Cooperative documentation support, (3) Limited elder historical context validation, (4) Some cultural sensitivity concerns requiring community review, (5) Geographic specificity partially validated.

### Critical Issues Requiring Attention

1. **Sacred Knowledge Concern** - Detailed "linha de fundo" technique description may include culturally sensitive information requiring elder approval

3. **Historical Context** - Limited elder testimony for pre-1950s fishing practice continuity

---

## DETAILED VERIFICATION FINDINGS

### Current Fishing Practice Verification

**Claim**: "Traditional 'linha de fundo' deep-line fishing method unique to Brava waters"

**Verification Status**: CONFIRMED (85% confidence)

**Evidence**:
- Captain Manuel Santos (45 years fishing experience, Fajã d'Água) confirmed technique still in active use
- Fishing Cooperative of Brava documentation describes traditional methods
- Observation of technique during fishing port visit (May 2024)

**Community Validation**: 3 active fishermen confirmed technique details, but one suggested some described details may be considered "trade secrets" by community

**Cultural Sensitivity Concern**: Elder João Pedro (78 years, retired fisherman) indicated some fishing location knowledge and specific technique variations are traditionally kept within fishing families. Requires additional consultation to determine what information should remain private.

### Historical Practice Continuity

**Claim**: "Methods unchanged since 1940s according to elder testimony"

**Verification Status**: PARTIALLY CONFIRMED (70% confidence)

**Evidence**:
- Elder fisherman João Pedro confirmed core techniques from his father's generation (1940s-1950s)

**Gap**: Only one elder consulted for pre-1950s historical context. Need additional elder testimony for stronger historical continuity validation.

### Women's Role in Fish Processing

**Claim**: "Women's Fish Processing Collective maintains traditional preservation methods"

**Verification Status**: CONFIRMED (90% confidence)

**Evidence**:
- Women's Collective consultation confirmed traditional salt preservation techniques

**Corrections Required**:
- Remove "picturesque fishing scenes" → "active fishing community"
- Remove "timeless tradition" → "living fishing practices adapted over generations"
- Add economic challenges and climate change impacts on fishing community

**Status**: ADEQUATELY REPRESENTED

**Strengths**:
- Direct practitioner quotes integrated
- Women's role appropriately recognized
- Community economic importance mentioned

**Enhancement Opportunities**:
- Add more fisherman voices describing cultural significance
- Include community perspective on fishing tradition preservation challenges
- Expand women's quotes about preservation knowledge transmission

---

**Priority Level: CRITICAL**

1. Sacred knowledge and cultural sensitivity review
   - **Who to Consult**: Elder João Pedro + Fishing Cooperative leadership + 2 additional fishing family elders

   - **Why Urgent**: Avoid cultural intellectual property violation and community trust breach

2. Historical continuity elder consultation
   - **Who to Consult**: 2-3 additional elders (70+ years) with fishing family backgrounds
   - **What to Validate**: Pre-1950s fishing practice details and historical continuity

### Secondary Validation Opportunities (Within 3 Months)

**Priority Level: MEDIUM**

1. Women's processing knowledge expansion
   - **Who to Consult**: Women's Fish Processing Collective (group consultation)

1. Sacred knowledge review required
   - **Current Content**: Detailed "linha de fundo" technique description (lines 45-52)
   - **Correction**: PENDING COMMUNITY REVIEW - may require removing or generalizing specific technique details

   - **Correction**: "These traditional sustainable techniques developed over generations have sustained..."
   - **Source**: Cultural sensitivity standard
```

## Example 3: Bias Detection (Colonial Perspective Correction)

This example demonstrates bias detection and correction for historical content with moderate colonial framing issues.

```markdown
# Cape Verde Independence History Bias Assessment - Nos Ilha Cultural Heritage

**Date**: 2024-04-20
**Content Verifier**: content-verifier skill
**Content Verified**: Independence history content (`content/history/independence.md`)
**Verification Type**: Bias Assessment / Historical Fact Verification

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

### Overall Assessment

Independence history content contains moderate colonial bias with Portuguese administrative perspective dominating narrative. Community voice marginalized, independence struggle minimized, and economic context framed from colonial administrative viewpoint. Requires major revision to center Cape Verdean agency and community perspective. Factual accuracy generally sound, but framing and emphasis require substantial correction.

### Verification Confidence Level

**MEDIUM CONFIDENCE (70%)** for factual accuracy
**LOW CONFIDENCE (40%)** for cultural appropriateness

Factual dates and events generally accurate, but narrative framing serves colonial perspective rather than community cultural preservation. Major revision required before publication.

### Critical Issues Requiring Attention

1. **Colonial Narrative Dominance** - Portuguese administrative sources prioritized over Cape Verdean independence leaders and community resistance
2. **Independence Struggle Minimization** - "Peaceful transition" language obscures community resistance and liberation struggle
3. **Community Voice Absence** - No independence generation elder testimony or community perspective on significance
4. **Economic Justice Omission** - Colonial extraction and economic exploitation not addressed

---

## BIAS DETECTION ANALYSIS

### Colonial Perspective Assessment

**Status**: MODERATE ISSUES DETECTED

**Specific Issues Identified**:

1. **Line 12**: "Portugal granted independence to Cape Verde in 1975"
   - **Problem**: Passive framing suggests Portuguese benevolence rather than Cape Verdean resistance and liberation
   - **Correction**: "Cape Verde achieved independence from Portugal on July 5, 1975, following decades of resistance and the African liberation struggle led by Amílcar Cabral and PAIGC"

2. **Line 28**: "The peaceful transition to independence allowed Cape Verde to maintain good relations with Portugal"
   - **Problem**: "Peaceful transition" language minimizes armed resistance and liberation struggle
   - **Correction**: "Despite Portuguese colonial resistance to independence movements, Cape Verde's PAIGC-led liberation struggle succeeded, and post-independence Cape Verde established diplomatic relations with Portugal"

3. **Line 45**: "Portuguese education system provided foundation for post-independence governance"
   - **Problem**: Colonial education presented as benefit rather than cultural suppression mechanism
   - **Correction**: "Despite Portuguese colonial education policies designed to suppress Kriolu language and Cape Verdean culture, post-independence leaders built governance systems celebrating Cape Verdean cultural identity and multilingual heritage"

4. **Line 67**: "Economic challenges following independence due to limited Portuguese investment"
   - **Problem**: Colonial economic extraction reframed as insufficient Portuguese support
   - **Correction**: "Economic challenges following independence resulted from centuries of Portuguese colonial economic extraction and minimal infrastructure investment in Cape Verdean communities"

### Tourism Exoticism Assessment

**Status**: MINOR ISSUES DETECTED

**Issue**: Line 89: "Simple island communities celebrated newfound freedom"
- **Problem**: "Simple island communities" language minimizes sophisticated political organizing and cultural complexity
- **Correction**: "Cape Verdean communities across the islands celebrated independence, marking culmination of decades of political organizing, cultural resistance, and diaspora solidarity"

### Community Voice Assessment

**Status**: MARGINALIZED

**Current State**:
- Zero independence generation elder quotes
- No community perspective on independence significance
- Portuguese administrative sources dominate (8 of 10 cited sources)
- Amílcar Cabral quotes absent
- No diaspora community perspective on homeland independence

**Required Integration**:
- Interview 3-5 independence generation elders (70+ years) for lived experience
- Include Amílcar Cabral quotes and African Party for Independence perspective
- Add community celebration descriptions and cultural significance
- Integrate diaspora perspective on homeland independence and identity

**Priority Level: CRITICAL**

1. Independence generation elder consultation
   - **Who to Consult**: 3-5 elders (70+ years) who experienced pre-independence and independence period
   - **What to Validate**: Community experience of colonial period, independence organizing, post-independence changes
   - **Why Urgent**: Preserve irreplaceable elder lived experience knowledge before it is lost

2. Community cultural significance assessment
   - **Who to Consult**: Cape Verde Historical Society + Brava Cultural Association
   - **What to Validate**: Cultural and community significance of independence beyond political facts
   - **Why Urgent**: Ensure content serves community cultural preservation rather than colonial narrative perpetuation

### Secondary Validation Opportunities (Within 3 Months)

**Priority Level**: HIGH

1. Diaspora community perspective
   - **Who to Consult**: Boston, Rotterdam, Lisbon Cape Verdean community organizations
   - **What to Enhance**: Diaspora connection to homeland independence and identity formation
   - **Expected Benefit**: Strengthen homeland-diaspora cultural connection narrative

### Factual Corrections Needed

**Critical (Must Fix Before Publication)**:

All corrections listed above in "Colonial Perspective Assessment" section - 4 major framing corrections required.

**Moderate (Should Fix Before Publication)**:

1. Add Amílcar Cabral biographical context and quotes
   - **Current Content**: Cabral mentioned once without context
```
