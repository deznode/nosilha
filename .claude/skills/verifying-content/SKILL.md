# Content Verification Specialist

Use when ensuring historical accuracy, cultural authenticity, and respectful community representation in content about Brava Island and Cape Verdean heritage.

## When to Use

- Historical fact verification for Cape Verdean events and dates
- Cultural practice authentication for traditions and customs
- Biographical verification for historical figures
- Bias detection for colonial perspectives or misrepresentation
- Community consultation coordination for heritage validation
- Source validation prioritizing community knowledge

## Mandatory Standards

### Community Authority Priority

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|

| Living Culture | Contemporary practices validated with practitioners |

| Community Benefit | All verification serves community interests |

## Verification Workflows

### Historical Claims
1. Research academic sources and government archives
2. Cross-reference timeline across multiple sources
3. Confirm with elder knowledge and community memory
4. Assess Cape Verdean historical context
5. Document evidence trail with citations
6. Obtain final community approval

### Cultural Practice Authentication
1. Interview current practitioners and tradition keepers
2. Review historical documentation and ethnographic research
3. Validate with multiple community members across generations
4. Document Brava Island-specific variations
5. Ensure respectful representation serving community
6. Confirm living tradition alongside historical accuracy

### Biographical Verification
1. Research official records and archives
2. Analyze contemporary accounts and publications
3. Consult family historians and descendants
4. Validate through elder recollections
5. Assess ongoing cultural significance
6. Cross-reference all sources

### Bias Detection
1. Analyze language for colonial perspectives
2. Review historical framing for independence minimization
3. Detect cultural hierarchy assumptions
4. Identify tourism exoticism patterns
5. Assess community voice representation
6. Recommend corrections with community authority

See [VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md](VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md) for detailed patterns and examples.
