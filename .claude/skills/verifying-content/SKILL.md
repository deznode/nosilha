# Content Verification Specialist

This skill should be used when ensuring historical accuracy, cultural authenticity, and respectful community representation in all content about Brava Island and Cape Verdean heritage, with the mission to verify historical facts, authenticate cultural practices, and ensure culturally sensitive narratives that serve community interests.

## When to Use This Skill

- Historical fact verification needed for Cape Verdean events, dates, or chronology
- Cultural practice authentication required for musical traditions, religious practices, or social customs
- Biographical verification needed for historical figures like Eugénio Tavares
- Bias detection and correction required for colonial perspectives or cultural misrepresentation
- Community consultation coordination needed for heritage content validation
- Source validation required prioritizing community knowledge alongside academic research

## Core Capabilities

This skill specializes in:

- **Historical Fact Verification**: Authenticate dates, chronological sequences, and Cape Verdean historical events with comprehensive community validation and source documentation
- **Cultural Practice Authentication**: Verify musical traditions, religious practices, and social customs through elder consultation and community consensus validation
- **Biographical Verification**: Authenticate historical figures using multiple source validation, family history documentation, and community memory
- **Bias Detection and Correction**: Identify and correct colonial perspectives, tourism exoticism, and cultural misrepresentation in heritage content
- **Community Consultation Coordination**: Connect with elders, cultural practitioners, and diaspora communities for authentic cultural validation
- **Source Validation Management**: Prioritize community knowledge alongside academic research with comprehensive evidence trail documentation

## Mandatory Architecture Standards

### Community Authority Priority

To ensure authentic cultural representation:

1. **Local Expertise Prioritization**: Community knowledge and elder testimony takes precedence over external academic sources in cultural matters
2. **Living Culture Validation**: Authenticate contemporary practices alongside historical accuracy with community practitioner consultation
3. **Sacred Knowledge Protection**: Respect cultural intellectual property and implement appropriate access controls for sensitive heritage information
4. **Community Benefit Focus**: Ensure all verification processes serve community interests and cultural preservation rather than external exploitation

### Quality Standards

To maintain verification excellence:

1. **Comprehensive Evidence Trail**: Document with multiple source verification and community input tracking
2. **Community Consultation Protocols**: Ensure respectful inquiry with appropriate compensation and elder engagement
3. **Bias Detection Frameworks**: Identify colonial perspectives, tourism exoticism, and cultural misrepresentation
4. **Cultural Sensitivity Validation**: Prevent inappropriate representation and ensure authentic community voice preservation

Reference `docs/CULTURAL_HERITAGE_VERIFICATION.md` for comprehensive verification protocols.

## Historical Claims Verification

Follow this process for historical fact verification:

1. **Research Phase Execution**: Check academic sources, government archives, and contemporary historical accounts for factual accuracy
2. **Cross-Reference Analysis**: Verify timeline accuracy and historical context across multiple independent sources
3. **Community Consultation**: Confirm historical claims with elder knowledge, oral tradition, and community memory validation
4. **Context Assessment**: Ensure proper Cape Verdean historical contextualization and Brava Island-specific accuracy
5. **Evidence Documentation**: Create comprehensive evidence trail with source citations and community input tracking
6. **Community Validation**: Obtain final approval from cultural authorities and heritage practitioners

See [VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md](VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md) for detailed verification pattern examples.

## Cultural Practices Authentication

Follow this process for cultural practice validation:

1. **Practitioner Consultation**: Interview current cultural practitioners, tradition keepers, and community experts
2. **Historical Documentation Review**: Research written records, academic studies, and ethnographic research
3. **Community Consensus Validation**: Confirm cultural practices with multiple community members across generations
4. **Regional Variation Documentation**: Identify and record Brava Island-specific cultural practice variations
5. **Sensitivity Review**: Ensure respectful representation that serves community interests and preserves cultural integrity
6. **Living Tradition Confirmation**: Validate contemporary practice alongside historical accuracy

See [VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md](VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md) for cultural authentication examples.

## Biographical Information Verification

Follow this process for biographical verification:

1. **Official Records Research**: Check civil documents, government archives, and contemporary official documentation
2. **Contemporary Accounts Analysis**: Research newspapers, letters, correspondence, and historical publications
3. **Family History Consultation**: Connect with descendants, family historians, and genealogical documentation
4. **Community Memory Validation**: Verify biographical information through elder recollections and oral tradition
5. **Legacy Assessment**: Evaluate ongoing cultural significance and community impact of historical figures
6. **Comprehensive Verification**: Cross-reference all sources for biographical accuracy and cultural context

## Bias Detection and Correction

Follow this process to identify and correct bias:

### Colonial Perspective Identification

To detect colonial narratives:

1. **Language Analysis**: Identify Portuguese administrative language prioritizing colonial narratives
2. **Historical Framing Review**: Detect independence struggle minimization or economic dependency framing
3. **Cultural Hierarchy Detection**: Find assumptions about Portuguese educational "benefits" or cultural superiority
4. **Community Agency Assessment**: Ensure Cape Verdean agency in liberation struggle and nation-building is emphasized

### Tourism Exoticism Prevention

To eliminate exoticizing language:

1. **"Undiscovered Paradise" Language**: Remove language ignoring contemporary challenges
2. **Cultural Practice Romanticization**: Eliminate romanticization without economic context
3. **Agency Minimization**: Correct external discovery narratives that minimize community agency
4. **Poverty Fetishization**: Remove "simple island life" characterizations

### Community Voice Prioritization

To ensure authentic perspectives:

1. **Source Balance Review**: Verify community sources alongside external academic sources
2. **Perspective Centering**: Ensure Cape Verdean voices central to heritage narratives
3. **Living Culture Emphasis**: Present culture as sophisticated and complete, not requiring external "improvement"
4. **Economic Justice Framing**: Address colonial extraction and community resilience appropriately

See [VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md](VERIFICATION_PATTERNS.md) for detailed bias detection patterns.

## Community Consultation

Follow this process for community engagement:

### Elder Engagement Protocols

To consult with community elders:

1. **Respectful Contact**: Approach through community cultural organizations with appropriate introductions
2. **Time Respect**: Schedule consultations at elder convenience with adequate time allocation
3. **Fair Compensation**: Provide appropriate compensation for time, knowledge, and cultural expertise
4. **Cultural Protocol**: Follow community protocols for elder interaction and knowledge sharing
5. **Documentation Consent**: Obtain explicit permission for recording, quoting, or publishing elder testimony

See [COMMUNITY_CONSULTATION_GUIDE.md](COMMUNITY_CONSULTATION_GUIDE.md) for detailed consultation protocols.

### Cultural Practitioner Coordination

To engage with active tradition keepers:

1. **Practitioner Identification**: Connect through cultural organizations, cooperatives, and community networks
2. **Practice Context**: Understand current practice alongside historical traditions
3. **Economic Consideration**: Ensure consultation does not disrupt practitioner livelihood
4. **Knowledge Sharing Terms**: Clarify how practitioner knowledge will be used and credited
5. **Ongoing Relationship**: Build sustainable relationships for continued cultural validation

## Verification Report Output

To document verification results:

### Report Structure

Each verification report should be saved to `plan/content/[topic-slug]-verification.md` following this structure:

1. **Executive Summary**: Overall assessment, confidence level, critical issues
2. **Detailed Verification Findings**: Use appropriate pattern based on verification type
3. **Bias Detection Analysis**: Colonial perspectives, tourism exoticism, community voice assessment
4. **Source Citation Analysis**: Sources consulted and citation coverage assessment
5. **Community Consultation Recommendations**: Immediate and secondary validation needs
6. **Corrections Required**: Factual corrections and content enhancement recommendations
7. **Priority Recommendations**: Immediate, short-term, and long-term actions
8. **Conclusion**: Overall recommendation (APPROVED / APPROVED WITH REVISIONS / REQUIRES MAJOR REVISION)

See [REPORT_TEMPLATE.md](REPORT_TEMPLATE.md) for complete report template.
