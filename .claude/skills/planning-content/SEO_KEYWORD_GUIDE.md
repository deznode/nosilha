# SEO Keyword Guide for Diaspora Discovery

This guide provides keyword research patterns for optimizing cultural heritage content to help Cape Verdean diaspora discover their ancestral homeland.

## Keyword Research Philosophy

**Primary Goal**: Help Cape Verdean diaspora find authentic Brava Island cultural heritage content when searching for their roots.

**Balance**: Cultural authenticity first, SEO optimization second. Keywords must integrate naturally within authentic cultural narratives.

## Diaspora Discovery Keyword Categories

### 1. Geographic + Cultural Identity Keywords

**Pattern**: `[Location] + [Cultural Element]`

Examples:
- "Brava Island Cape Verde"
- "Cape Verde traditions"
- "Brava Island culture"
- "Nova Sintra Brava"
- "Cape Verdean heritage"

**Search Intent**: Diaspora seeking information about their ancestral island and cultural background.

**Usage**: Primary keywords for educational heritage pages, directory category pages.

### 2. Cultural Practice Keywords

**Pattern**: `[Traditional Practice] + Cape Verde/Brava`

Examples:
- "morna music Cape Verde"
- "cachupa recipe traditional"
- "Cape Verdean dance traditions"
- "Brava Island festivals"
- "traditional Cape Verdean food"

**Search Intent**: Learning about specific cultural traditions, often for cultural connection or event planning.

**Usage**: Topic-specific heritage pages, cultural practice guides.

### 3. Cultural Term Definition Keywords

**Pattern**: `[Kriolu/Cultural Term] + meaning/definition`

Examples:
- "morabeza meaning"
- "sodade Cape Verde"
- "what is morna music"
- "Kriolu language"
- "Cape Verde national dish"

**Search Intent**: Diaspora or learners seeking to understand cultural terms they've heard from family.

**Usage**: FAQ schema, cultural glossary pages, educational content with embedded definitions.

### 4. Heritage Connection Keywords

**Pattern**: `[Heritage Activity] + Cape Verde/Brava`

Examples:
- "Cape Verde ancestry"
- "Brava Island genealogy"
- "Cape Verdean family history"
- "visiting ancestral homeland"
- "Cape Verde heritage tourism"

**Search Intent**: Diaspora planning heritage trips or researching family roots.

**Usage**: Diaspora connection content, heritage travel planning pages.

### 5. Notable Figures Keywords

**Pattern**: `[Name] + Cape Verde/Brava + [Role/Achievement]`

Examples:
- "Eugénio Tavares poet"
- "famous Cape Verdean musicians"
- "Brava Island historical figures"
- "Cape Verde cultural icons"
- "morna composers"

**Search Intent**: Learning about cultural heroes, often for cultural education or pride.

**Usage**: Biography pages, cultural heritage history content.

### 6. Community + Diaspora Keywords

**Pattern**: `Cape Verdean + [Diaspora Location/Community]`

Examples:
- "Cape Verdean community Boston"
- "Cape Verdean diaspora Portugal"
- "Cape Verde cultural events France"
- "Cape Verdean Americans"
- "Cape Verde expat community"

**Search Intent**: Diaspora seeking cultural community connections in their current location.

**Usage**: Diaspora bridge content, community connection pages.

## Keyword Density Guidelines

**Target Density**: 0.5-1.5% for primary keywords

**Calculation**: (Number of keyword instances / Total word count) × 100

**Example**: For 1,500-word article:
- Primary keyword: 7-22 instances (0.5-1.5% density)
- Natural distribution: Introduction, headings, body paragraphs, conclusion

**Best Practices**:
- Use exact match primary keyword 3-5 times
- Use semantic variations 5-10 times
- Use related cultural terms naturally throughout
- Never force keyword density at expense of cultural authenticity

## Semantic Keyword Variations

Use semantic variations to avoid repetitive keyword stuffing:

**Example for "morna music"**:
- Exact match: "morna music"
- Variations: "traditional morna", "morna songs", "morna musical tradition", "morna genre", "Cape Verdean morna"

**Example for "Brava Island"**:
- Exact match: "Brava Island"
- Variations: "the island of Brava", "Brava", "Ilha Brava", "this Cape Verdean island"

**Example for "Cape Verdean culture"**:
- Exact match: "Cape Verdean culture"
- Variations: "Cape Verde cultural heritage", "cultural traditions of Cape Verde", "Cape Verdean cultural practices", "island culture"

## E-E-A-T Signal Keywords

### Experience Keywords

Signal first-hand cultural experience:

- "born and raised in [location]"
- "local [profession/role]"
- "family tradition of [practice]"
- "generations of [cultural practice]"
- "community members who [activity]"

**Example**: "João Monteiro, born and raised in Nova Sintra, continues his family's five-generation tradition of morna performance."

### Expertise Keywords

Signal cultural authority:

- "cultural expert"
- "tradition keeper"
- "historian"
- "master [craftsperson/artist]"
- "recognized authority on [topic]"

**Example**: "According to Maria Silva, a recognized authority on Brava Island culinary traditions..."

### Authoritativeness Keywords

Signal credible sources:

- "UNESCO recognized"
- "documented by [institution]"
- "research by [academic/cultural institution]"
- "official records show"
- "according to [authoritative source]"

**Example**: "UNESCO recognition of morna as Intangible Cultural Heritage affirms its global cultural significance."

### Trust Keywords

Signal community validation:

- "community approved"
- "validated by elders"
- "family testimonial"
- "local community consensus"
- "resident perspectives"

**Example**: "Community elders have validated this account of Brava's settlement history."

## Common Keyword Mistakes to Avoid

### 1. Tourism Clichés

**Avoid**:
- "exotic Cape Verde"
- "hidden gem"
- "off the beaten path"
- "undiscovered paradise"

**Why**: Othering language, external observer perspective

**Use Instead**: Authentic cultural descriptions with specific community examples

### 2. Keyword Stuffing

**Avoid**: "Brava Island Cape Verde is the most beautiful island in Cape Verde. When visiting Brava Island Cape Verde, you'll discover Brava Island's unique Cape Verde culture."

**Why**: Unnatural, damages readability, penalized by search engines

**Use Instead**: Natural keyword integration within cultural narrative

### 3. Irrelevant High-Volume Keywords

**Avoid**: Targeting "Caribbean islands" or "African beaches" for Brava content

**Why**: Wrong audience, not diaspora-focused, culturally inaccurate

**Use Instead**: Specific Cape Verdean diaspora discovery keywords even if lower volume

### 4. Cultural Inaccuracy for SEO

**Avoid**: Describing morna as "Cape Verdean blues" just for keyword matching

**Why**: Cultural authenticity must never be compromised for SEO

**Use Instead**: Authentic cultural description with natural keyword integration
