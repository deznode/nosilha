# Content Planning Examples

Real-world examples demonstrating content planning workflows for Brava Island cultural heritage.

## Example 1: Cultural Heritage Educational Page

**Request**: "Plan content for a page about morna music and Brava Island's musical heritage"

**Phase 1 - Cultural Context**:
- Cultural significance: Morna as Brava's defining art form, UNESCO intangible heritage
- Community perspectives: Living musicians, festival organizers, music teachers
- Morabeza integration: Emotional depth of sodade, hospitality through music
- Diaspora connection: Morna as bridge to homeland for global Cape Verdeans

**Phase 2 - SEO Strategy**:
- Primary keyword: "morna music Cape Verde" (informational intent)
- Secondary: "Brava Island music", "Eugénio Tavares", "Cape Verdean songs"
- E-E-A-T signals: Local musician quotes, historical citations, UNESCO reference

**Phase 3 - Content Outline** (1,800 words):
```
1. Introduction (150 words): Hook with morna's emotional power
2. Cultural Significance (400 words): Community role, sodade expression
3. Historical Context (500 words): Eugénio Tavares legacy, evolution
4. Contemporary Practice (400 words): Today's musicians, festivals
5. Diaspora Connection (250 words): Morna in diaspora communities
6. Conclusion (100 words): Cultural invitation, morabeza closing
```

**Output**: Saved to `plan/content/morna-music-brava-heritage.md`

## Example 2: Directory Entry with Heritage Context

**Request**: "Plan content for a traditional restaurant directory entry"

**Phase 1 - Cultural Context**:
- Cultural significance: Traditional cachupa, family recipes, community gathering
- Community perspective: Owner story, generational recipes, local ingredients
- Heritage context: How food connects to Cape Verdean identity

**Phase 2 - SEO Strategy**:
- Primary keyword: "[Restaurant Name] Brava Island"
- Secondary: "traditional Cape Verdean restaurant", "cachupa Brava"
- Local SEO: Accurate coordinates, Google Business alignment

**Phase 3 - Content Outline** (400-600 words):
```
1. Overview (100 words): Location, atmosphere, heritage context
2. Culinary Heritage (150 words): Traditional dishes, recipe origins
3. Owner Story (100 words): Family history, community connection
4. Signature Dishes (150 words): Cachupa, grogue, local specialties
5. Visitor Information (100 words): Hours, reservations, location
```

**Output**: Saved to `plan/content/directory/[restaurant-slug].md`

## Example 3: Diaspora Connection Content

**Request**: "Plan content about family heritage discovery for diaspora visitors"

**Phase 1 - Cultural Context**:
- Cultural significance: Reconnection with ancestral homeland
- Community perspective: Local families, genealogy resources, community welcome
- Sodade integration: Longing fulfilled, homecoming emotion
- Diaspora bridge: Practical guidance + emotional preparation

**Phase 2 - SEO Strategy**:
- Primary keyword: "Cape Verde family heritage" (informational/navigational)
- Secondary: "Brava Island ancestry", "Cape Verdean genealogy", "visit homeland"
- Intent: Diaspora planning ancestral visit

**Phase 3 - Content Outline** (1,600 words):
```
1. Introduction (150 words): Emotional significance of homecoming
2. Preparing for Your Visit (350 words): Documents, family research, expectations
3. Finding Family in Brava (400 words): Local resources, community connections
4. Cultural Etiquette (300 words): Morabeza expectations, gift-giving, respect
5. Emotional Journey (250 words): Managing sodade, embracing connection
6. Conclusion (150 words): Community welcome, planning next steps
```

**Cultural Verification Checkpoints**:
- Community validation: Local family connection protocols
- Sensitivity: Privacy of family information
- Accuracy: Genealogy resource availability

**Output**: Saved to `plan/content/diaspora-family-heritage-visit.md`

```markdown

- Significance to Brava community
- Community perspectives to include
- Morabeza integration approach
- Diaspora connection points

- Secondary keywords
- E-E-A-T signals planned

## Content Outline
- Section-by-section breakdown with word counts
- Key points per section

- Claims requiring verification
- Community validation needs
- Sensitivity considerations

## Multilingual Notes

- French adaptation priorities

## Handoff Requirements
- Content creator guidance

- Visual content needs
```
