# Cultural Heritage Content Planner

Creates detailed content strategy plans for Brava Island cultural heritage that prioritize Cape Verdean authenticity while optimizing for global diaspora discovery.

## When to Use

- Content planning for Brava Island cultural heritage topics
- Educational page planning for traditions, music, practices, history
- Diaspora connection content bridging global community with homeland
- Directory entry planning with culturally-rich heritage context
- Content strategy requiring cultural verification checkpoints
- Multilingual planning across English, Portuguese, French

## Content Types

| Type | Focus | Share |
|------|-------|-------|
| Heritage Educational Pages | Traditions, morna, cultural practices, history | 80% |
| Diaspora Connection | Sodade, family heritage, homeland bridge | 15% |
| Directory Entries | Restaurants, hotels, landmarks with context | 5% |

## Planning Approach

1. **Cultural Authenticity First**: Prioritize authentic representation, community voice, morabeza spirit
2. **SEO Optimization Second**: Optimize for diaspora discovery after cultural foundation
3. **Multilingual Strategy**: English (primary) with adaptation notes for Portuguese/French
4. **Verification Planning**: Include accuracy checkpoints and community validation

Reference `docs/design-system.md` and `docs/cultural-heritage-verification.md`.

## Five-Phase Framework

### Phase 1: Cultural Context Analysis
1. Research cultural significance to Brava community
2. Identify authentic resident voices and perspectives
3. Plan morabeza spirit integration
4. Map diaspora connections and homeland links
5. Identify verification checkpoints per `docs/cultural-heritage-verification.md`

### Phase 2: SEO Strategy
1. Research diaspora discovery keywords
2. Identify cultural term keywords (morabeza, sodade, morna)
3. Analyze search intent (informational, connection, planning)
4. Plan E-E-A-T signals (experience, expertise, authority, trust)

See [SEO_KEYWORD_GUIDE.md](SEO_KEYWORD_GUIDE.md) for detailed patterns.

### Phase 3: Content Outline
Create structure (target 1,400-2,000 words):
- Introduction: 100-150 words (cultural hook, value prop)
- Cultural Significance: 300-400 words (community perspective, living tradition)
- Historical Context: 400-500 words (verified background, evolution)
- Contemporary Practice: 300-400 words (how tradition lives today)
- Diaspora Connection: 200-300 words (global engagement, sodade)
- Conclusion: 100-150 words (summary, CTA, morabeza closing)

### Phase 4: Multilingual Adaptation
- English: 8th-10th grade, define cultural terms, pronunciation guides
- Portuguese: Kriolu integration, Cape Verdean dialect, homeland focus
- French: West African connections, francophone diaspora bridges

### Phase 5: Schema & Technical SEO
Plan structured data (Article, Place, Event, FAQ) and meta optimization.

See [CONTENT_PLAN_TEMPLATE.md](CONTENT_PLAN_TEMPLATE.md) for complete template.

## Content Plan Output

Save plans to: `plan/content/[topic-slug].md`

Example: `plan/content/morna-music-brava-heritage.md`

1. Authenticity over optimization: If conflict, prioritize cultural authenticity
2. Living tradition: Present culture as dynamic, not frozen artifact
3. Community benefit: Plan content serving Brava Island community
4. Diaspora bridge: Facilitate meaningful homeland connections
5. Cultural boundaries: Respect sacred practices, require community consent
