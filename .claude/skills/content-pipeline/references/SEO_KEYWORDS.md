# SEO Keyword Guide for Diaspora Discovery

Optimize cultural heritage content to help Cape Verdean diaspora discover their ancestral homeland.

**Philosophy**: Cultural authenticity first, SEO optimization second. Keywords must integrate naturally within authentic cultural narratives.

## Keyword Categories

### 1. Geographic + Cultural Identity
Pattern: `[Location] + [Cultural Element]`
- "Brava Island Cape Verde", "Cape Verde traditions", "Nova Sintra Brava"
- Intent: Diaspora seeking ancestral island information

### 2. Cultural Practice
Pattern: `[Traditional Practice] + Cape Verde/Brava`
- "morna music Cape Verde", "cachupa recipe traditional", "Cape Verdean dance traditions"
- Intent: Learning about specific cultural traditions

### 3. Cultural Term Definitions
Pattern: `[Kriolu/Cultural Term] + meaning/definition`
- "morabeza meaning", "sodade Cape Verde", "what is morna music"
- Intent: Understanding terms heard from family. Good FAQ schema candidates.

### 4. Heritage Connection
Pattern: `[Heritage Activity] + Cape Verde/Brava`
- "Cape Verde ancestry", "Brava Island genealogy", "visiting ancestral homeland"
- Intent: Diaspora planning heritage trips or researching family roots

### 5. Notable Figures
Pattern: `[Name] + Cape Verde/Brava + [Role]`
- "Eugenio Tavares poet", "famous Cape Verdean musicians"
- Intent: Learning about cultural heroes

### 6. Diaspora Community
Pattern: `Cape Verdean + [Diaspora Location/Community]`
- "Cape Verdean community Boston", "Cape Verdean diaspora Portugal"
- Intent: Seeking cultural community connections in current location

## Keyword Density

- Target: 0.5-1.5% for primary keywords
- Exact match primary keyword: 3-5 times
- Semantic variations: 5-10 times
- Never force density at expense of cultural authenticity

### Semantic Variation Examples
- "morna music" → "traditional morna", "morna songs", "morna musical tradition", "Cape Verdean morna"
- "Brava Island" → "the island of Brava", "Ilha Brava", "this Cape Verdean island"

## E-E-A-T Signal Keywords

| Signal | Pattern | Example |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Experience | "born and raised in", "family tradition of" | "Joao Monteiro, born and raised in Nova Sintra" |
| Expertise | "cultural expert", "tradition keeper", "master" | "recognized authority on Brava Island culinary traditions" |
| Authority | "UNESCO recognized", "documented by", "research by" | "UNESCO recognition of morna as Intangible Cultural Heritage" |
| Trust | "community approved", "validated by elders" | "Community elders have validated this account" |

## Keywords to Avoid

- Tourism cliches: "exotic", "hidden gem", "off the beaten path", "undiscovered paradise"
- Othering language: "the locals", "primitive", "simple island life"
- Inaccurate associations: "Caribbean islands", "African beaches" (culturally inaccurate for Cape Verde)
- Forced cultural comparisons: "Cape Verdean blues" for morna (compromises authenticity)
