## Elder Engagement

### Initial Contact
1. Contact Brava Cultural Association or Nova Sintra Cultural Center for introduction
2. Explain verification purpose and community benefit in Portuguese/Kriolu
3. Seek elders with recognized cultural authority
4. Include geographic diversity (Nova Sintra, Faja d'Agua, Nossa Senhora do Monte)
5. Include both men and women for balanced perspective

### Consultation Best Practices
- Greet with traditional Cape Verdean respect (handshake, title use)
- Begin with social conversation before cultural topics
- Accept offered refreshments (coffee, grogue) as cultural courtesy
- Follow elder lead on language preference (Portuguese, Kriolu, or mixed)
- Ask open-ended questions allowing narrative response
- Allow time for reflection and memory processing
- Monitor elder energy levels, offer breaks, conclude at first signs of fatigue

### Fair Compensation
- **Minimum**: 2,000 CVE ($20 USD) for 1-2 hour consultation
- **Extended**: 3,000-5,000 CVE ($30-50 USD) for longer sessions
- Payment in cash (CVE) at consultation conclusion
- Alternative: grocery items, transportation assistance, copies of published content
- Community benefit: donations to cultural organizations, support for heritage projects

### Consent and Documentation
- Request permission before recording (audio, video, or notes-only)
- Obtain explicit quotation and attribution permission
- Clarify statements elder prefers off-record
- Provide draft content for elder review before publication
- Identify culturally sensitive information and implement access controls

## Cultural Practitioner Coordination

### Finding Practitioners
- **Cultural organizations**: Brava Cultural Association, Nova Sintra Cultural Center
- **Cooperatives**: Fishing Cooperative of Brava, Women's Collectives
- **Community networks**: Church organizations, family networks, local businesses
- **Direct outreach**: Cultural events, traditional practice locations, family referrals

### Practice Documentation
- Observe practice in authentic context (not staged demonstrations)
- Document contemporary details and variations
- Understand economic importance and sustainability
- Confirm practice longevity through elder testimony
- Distinguish core tradition from adaptive variation
- Record Brava Island-specific variations

### Compensation
- Pay at or above normal work rate for practitioner time
- Reimburse materials used in demonstration
- Avoid scheduling during peak work periods
- Protect practitioner intellectual property rights

## Diaspora Community Engagement

### Key Diaspora Communities
- Boston Cape Verdean Historical Society (USA)
- New Bedford Cape Verdean community organizations (USA)
- Pawtucket/Providence Cape Verdean associations (Rhode Island)
- Rotterdam Cape Verdean cultural centers (Netherlands)
- Lisbon Cape Verdean community groups (Portugal)

### Virtual Consultation
- WhatsApp video calls (widely used by Cape Verdean diaspora)
- Zoom/Google Meet for group consultations
- Follow same compensation and consent protocols as in-person

## Community Validation Workflow

1. **Circulate draft** to consulted elders and practitioners
2. **Allow 2-3 weeks** for review and feedback
3. **Provide translation** (Portuguese/Kriolu) if needed
4. **Integrate feedback**: Incorporate corrections, address sensitivity concerns
5. **Obtain final approval** from primary cultural authorities
6. **Document approval** in verification report

## Consultation Checklist

### Pre-Consultation
- [ ] Cultural organization introduction obtained
- [ ] Consent for consultation confirmed
- [ ] Scheduled at community member convenience
- [ ] Compensation prepared (cash CVE)
- [ ] Recording equipment tested (if permission obtained)
- [ ] Background research completed

### During Consultation
- [ ] Traditional greeting and social conversation
- [ ] Recording permission confirmed
- [ ] Open-ended questions with time for reflection
- [ ] Elder comfort and energy monitored
- [ ] Sensitive knowledge identified and protected
- [ ] Compensation provided at conclusion

### Post-Consultation
- [ ] Thank you communication sent
- [ ] Notes transcribed and organized
- [ ] Draft provided for community review
- [ ] Feedback integrated
- [ ] Final approval documented
- [ ] Ongoing relationship maintained
