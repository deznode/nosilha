# Web Research Examples

Real-world examples demonstrating research workflows for cultural heritage content.

## Example 1: Historical Timeline Research

**Request**: "Research the history of Nova Sintra settlement on Brava Island"

**Process**:
1. Query formulation: "Nova Sintra Brava Island history", "Brava Cape Verde settlement timeline", "Nova Sintra founded"
2. Domain filtering: Prioritize `.edu`, `.gov`, Wikipedia, academic sources
3. Cross-reference dates across 3+ sources
4. Identify contradictions and gaps

**Output**:
- Saved to: `plan/content/cultural-research/brava-history/Nova Sintra Settlement History_.md`
- Key findings: Settlement dates, founding families, architectural heritage
- Source quality: 4 academic sources, 2 government archives, Wikipedia baseline

## Example 2: Cultural Practice Verification

**Request**: "Verify information about morna music origins and Brava's connection to Eugénio Tavares"

**Process**:
1. Query variations: "Eugénio Tavares morna", "morna music Brava Island origin", "Cape Verde morna history"
2. Source prioritization: Academic musicology papers, Cape Verdean government cultural sites, UNESCO documentation
3. Fact triangulation: Birth/death dates, composition dates, cultural significance claims
4. Document contradictions in scholarly interpretations

**Output**:
- Saved to: `plan/content/cultural-research/brava-notable-figures/Eugenio Tavares Morna Research_.md`
- Key findings: Verified biographical data, composition catalog, cultural impact assessment
- Contradictions noted: Varying claims about "first morna" composition dates
- Sources cited: 6 sources with credibility ratings

## Example 3: Diaspora Community Research

**Request**: "Research Cape Verdean diaspora communities in New England for diaspora connection content"

**Process**:
1. Query strategy: "Cape Verdean community Massachusetts", "Brava immigrants New Bedford", "Cape Verde diaspora organizations USA"
2. Source types: Community organization websites, academic diaspora studies, census data, news archives
3. Cross-reference population statistics and community history
4. Identify active community organizations and events

**Output**:
- Saved to: `plan/content/cultural-research/Cape Verdean Diaspora New England_.md`
- Key findings: Historical immigration patterns, community population centers, active cultural organizations
- Source quality: Mix of academic (3), community organizations (4), government census (1)
- Follow-up recommended: Direct community outreach for first-hand perspectives
