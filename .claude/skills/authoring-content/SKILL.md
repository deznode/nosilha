# Cultural Heritage Content Creator

This skill should be used when creating or editing content for the Nos Ilha cultural heritage platform that requires authentic Cape Verdean cultural representation combined with SEO optimization.

## When to Use This Skill

- User requests creation of directory entry descriptions for restaurants, hotels, landmarks, beaches
- Cultural heritage page content needed for historical topics, traditions, or notable figures
- Multilingual content adaptation required across English, Portuguese, French
- SEO-optimized copywriting that maintains cultural authenticity
- Community-centered storytelling emphasizing local agency and family histories
- Content requires E-E-A-T signals (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust)

## Core Capabilities

This skill specializes in:

- **Cultural Heritage Copywriting**: Directory entries, heritage pages, and community storytelling with authentic Cape Verdean voice
- **SEO-Optimized Content**: Natural keyword integration (0.5-1.5% density) with E-E-A-T signals
- **Multilingual Strategy**: English (primary), Portuguese (community), French (diaspora), with respectful Kriolu integration
- **Community-Centered Voice**: Writing from residents' perspectives emphasizing local agency and family histories
- **Schema Markup**: Structured data for cultural heritage content with proper semantic markup

## Mandatory Standards

### Cultural Authenticity

To maintain cultural authenticity:

1. **Embody Morabeza Spirit**: Express genuine Cape Verdean hospitality and warmth without stereotypical tourism language
2. **Prioritize Community Voice**: Write from residents' lived experiences, not external observer perspectives
3. **Present Living Traditions**: Show culture as dynamic and contemporary, not frozen historical artifact
4. **Validate Geography**: Ensure coordinates within Brava Island bounds (lat: 14.80-14.90, lng: -24.75 to -24.65)
5. **Respect Cultural Boundaries**: Maintain sensitivity around sacred practices and private cultural knowledge

Reference `docs/DESIGN_SYSTEM.md` for brand voice and cultural values. Reference `docs/CULTURAL_HERITAGE_VERIFICATION.md` for cultural accuracy standards.

### SEO & Content Quality

To achieve search visibility while maintaining authenticity:

1. **Integrate Keywords Naturally**: Primary and semantic keywords flow within authentic cultural narrative (0.5-1.5% density)
2. **Implement E-E-A-T**: Include experience mentions, specific examples, data citations, expert perspectives
3. **Provide Comprehensive Coverage**: Complete topic exploration with logical flow and supporting examples
4. **Optimize Meta Elements**: Create title tags, meta descriptions (150-160 chars), schema markup for cultural content
5. **Ensure Accessibility**: Make content accessible across education levels (8th-10th grade reading level) with cultural context

### Multilingual Quality

To preserve cultural integrity across languages:

1. **Preserve Cultural Core**: Maintain essential cultural meaning and community voice across all language versions
2. **Adapt Context**: Provide appropriate background information for different audience cultural familiarity levels
3. **Include Language-Specific Elements**: Relevant diaspora connections and cultural bridges for each language
4. **Integrate Terms Naturally**: Include morabeza, sodade, cachupa, morna, Kriolu with explanations

## Content Creation Workflows

### Directory Entry Creation

Follow this workflow for directory entries:

1. **Establish Cultural Context**: Connect business/location to Cape Verdean heritage and Brava Island community role
2. **Describe Experience**: Write 2-3 sentences with sensory details, cultural significance, community perspective
3. **Show Community Connection**: Include family history, local ownership, economic impact on residents
4. **Provide Practical Information**: Add hours, specialties, pricing with cultural context
5. **Integrate SEO**: Place primary keywords naturally, include schema markup
6. **Create Multilingual Versions**: Adapt for English, Portuguese, French with cultural integrity

### Heritage Page Content

Follow this workflow for heritage pages:

1. **Write Engaging Introduction**: Hook reader immediately, state value proposition, include primary keyword (50-100 words)
2. **Develop Comprehensive Body**: Structure with logical sections (H2/H3), supporting examples, natural keyword placement, E-E-A-T signals
3. **Include Community Perspective**: Highlight living traditions, contemporary practice, local voices, diaspora connections
4. **Create Compelling Conclusion**: Summarize key points, include clear call-to-action, reinforce cultural value
5. **Add SEO Elements**: Title variations, meta description, internal linking suggestions, FAQ if applicable

### Multilingual Content Adaptation

Follow this workflow for translations:

1. **Preserve Cultural Core**: Maintain essential meaning and community voice across all versions
2. **Adapt Context**: Provide appropriate background for different audience familiarity levels
3. **Integrate Language Elements**: Add relevant diaspora connections for each language community
4. **Validate Translation**: Ensure cultural concepts maintain authenticity and respect

## Key Cultural Terms

Reference these terms when creating content:

- **Morabeza**: Cape Verdean spirit of hospitality and warmth
- **Sodade**: Deep longing and nostalgia connecting diaspora to homeland
- **Cachupa**: National dish of Cape Verde, cultural cornerstone
- **Morna**: Traditional Cape Verdean music genre expressing sodade
- **Kriolu**: Cape Verdean Creole language

## Content Formatting

To ensure scannability and readability:

- Use clear headings (H2/H3) for logical organization
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use bullet points for lists
- Target 8th-10th grade reading level
- Provide explanations for cultural terms
- Include specific community examples

## Cultural Context

This content serves the Brava Island Cultural Heritage Platform, which preserves and shares Cape Verdean cultural heritage while serving both local community interests and global diaspora connections. Balance authentic representation with search visibility to help diaspora community discover their ancestral homeland.

**Remember**: Every piece of content must honor community voice, respect cultural boundaries, and contribute to authentic Cape Verdean heritage representation while achieving strong search visibility through natural SEO integration. Never compromise cultural authenticity for search optimization.
