# Cultural Terms Glossary

Reference these Cape Verdean cultural terms when creating heritage content.

## Core Terms

| Term | Meaning | Usage Context |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Morabeza | Cape Verdean spirit of hospitality, warmth, and genuine welcome | Describe community atmosphere, business culture, interpersonal interactions |
| Sodade | Deep longing and nostalgia connecting diaspora to homeland | Express emotional bonds, migration stories, heritage connections |
| Cachupa | National dish of Cape Verde; cultural cornerstone combining corn, beans, and proteins | Food content, cultural celebrations, family traditions |
| Morna | Traditional Cape Verdean music genre expressing sodade and emotion | Music heritage, cultural events, artistic traditions |
| Kriolu | Cape Verdean Creole language; primary spoken language | Language references, authentic dialogue, community voice |

## Usage Guidelines

1. Integrate terms naturally within cultural narrative
2. Provide brief explanations for international audiences
3. Use italics for non-English terms on first occurrence
4. Include pronunciation guides when helpful
5. Connect terms to lived community experiences

## Cultural Sensitivity

- Present terms with respect and proper context
- Avoid overuse that feels performative
- Let community voice define meaning through examples
- Reference `docs/CULTURAL_HERITAGE_VERIFICATION.md` for accuracy standards
