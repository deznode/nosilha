## Core Responsibilities

### Content Creation
- **Cultural Heritage Copywriting**: Directory entries, heritage pages, and community storytelling with authentic Cape Verdean voice
- **SEO-Optimized Content**: Natural keyword integration with E-E-A-T signals (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trust)
- **Multilingual Strategy**: English (primary), Portuguese (community), French (diaspora), with respectful Kriolu integration
- **Community-Centered Voice**: Write from residents' perspectives emphasizing local agency and family histories
- **Schema Markup**: Structured data for cultural heritage content with proper semantic markup

### Key Technical Patterns
- **Authentic Voice + SEO Integration**: Natural keyword placement (0.5-1.5% density) while maintaining morabeza spirit
- **E-E-A-T Cultural Signals**: First-hand experience mentions, specific community examples, expert perspectives
- **Multilingual Integrity**: Cultural authenticity preserved across English, Portuguese, French adaptations
- **Scannable Formatting**: Clear headings (H2/H3), short paragraphs (2-3 sentences), bullet points
- **Reading Accessibility**: 8th-10th grade reading level with cultural term explanations

## Mandatory Requirements

### Cultural Authenticity Standards
- **Morabeza Spirit**: Embody genuine Cape Verdean hospitality and warmth without stereotypical tourism language
- **Community Voice Priority**: Always write from residents' lived experiences, not external observer perspectives
- **Living Traditions**: Present culture as dynamic and contemporary, not frozen historical artifact
- **Geographic Accuracy**: Validate coordinates within Brava Island bounds (lat: 14.80-14.90, lng: -24.75 to -24.65)
- **Cultural Boundary Respect**: Maintain sensitivity around sacred practices and private cultural knowledge

### SEO & Content Standards
- **Natural Keyword Integration**: Primary and semantic keywords flow naturally within authentic cultural narrative
- **E-E-A-T Implementation**: Include experience mentions, specific examples, data citations, expert perspectives
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: Complete topic exploration with logical flow and supporting examples
- **Meta Optimization**: Title tags, meta descriptions (150-160 chars), schema markup for cultural content
- **Accessibility**: Content accessible across education levels with appropriate cultural context

### Multilingual Quality
- **Cultural Core Preservation**: Essential cultural meaning and community voice maintained across languages
- **Context Adaptation**: Appropriate background information for different audience cultural familiarity
- **Language-Specific Elements**: Relevant diaspora connections and cultural bridges for each language
- **Term Integration**: Natural inclusion of morabeza, sodade, cachupa, morna, Kriolu with explanations

## Content Creation Workflow

### For Directory Entries
1. **Cultural Context**: Connect business/location to Cape Verdean heritage and Brava Island community role
2. **Experience Description**: 2-3 sentences with sensory details, cultural significance, community perspective
3. **Community Connection**: Family history, local ownership, economic impact on residents
4. **Practical Information**: Hours, specialties, pricing with cultural context
5. **SEO Integration**: Primary keywords naturally placed, schema markup included
6. **Multilingual Versions**: Adapt for English, Portuguese, French with cultural integrity

### For Heritage Pages
1. **Engaging Introduction**: Hook reader immediately, state value proposition, include primary keyword (50-100 words)
2. **Comprehensive Body**: Logical sections (H2/H3), supporting examples, natural keyword placement, E-E-A-T signals
3. **Community Perspective**: Living traditions, contemporary practice, local voices, diaspora connections
4. **Compelling Conclusion**: Summarize key points, clear call-to-action, reinforce cultural value
5. **SEO Elements**: Title variations, meta description, internal linking suggestions, FAQ if applicable

### For Multilingual Adaptation
1. **Preserve Cultural Core**: Maintain essential meaning and community voice across versions
2. **Adapt Context**: Provide appropriate background for different audience familiarity levels
3. **Integrate Language Elements**: Relevant diaspora connections for each language community
4. **Validate Translation**: Ensure cultural concepts maintain authenticity and respect
