# Nos Ilha Content Review & Enhancement Plan

## Executive Summary

This document provides a comprehensive plan for reviewing, fact-checking, and enhancing all content across the Nos Ilha platform. Based on analysis of the current 19 pages, this plan identifies specific areas requiring research, verification, and expansion to ensure the platform serves as an authentic and authoritative source for Brava Island's cultural heritage.

### Current Status Overview
- **19 main pages** analyzed across different content categories
- **Strong foundation** with well-structured content and good design
- **Critical gaps** in historical verification and cultural authenticity
- **Expansion opportunities** for comprehensive coverage of Brava Island

### Priority Areas
1. **Historical Accuracy** - Fact-check all dates, events, and biographical information
2. **Cultural Authenticity** - Verify traditions, customs, and cultural practices with community sources
3. **Geographic Precision** - Validate location data, populations, and geographic information
4. **Community Validation** - Establish connections with Brava community for ongoing verification

## Quality Assurance & Validation

### Fact-Checking Standards
1. **Multiple Source Verification** - Require at least 2 independent sources for historical claims
2. **Community Validation** - Have local community members review cultural content
3. **Academic Review** - Engage with Cape Verde researchers for historical accuracy
4. **Regular Updates** - Establish process for ongoing content maintenance

### Community Validation Process
1. **Local Reviewers** - Establish contact with Brava community leaders
2. **Diaspora Input** - Engage Cape Verdean communities worldwide
3. **Academic Advisors** - Connect with Cape Verde studies researchers
4. **Cultural Practitioners** - Include musicians, artists, and cultural leaders

### Source Documentation
1. **Citation System** - Properly cite all sources used
2. **Source Quality Assessment** - Evaluate reliability of all sources
3. **Update Tracking** - Maintain record of all content changes
4. **Transparency** - Make research process transparent to community
