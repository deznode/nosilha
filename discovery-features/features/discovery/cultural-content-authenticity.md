# Discovery: Cultural Content Authenticity Verification

**Status:** Discovery Phase  
**Priority:** High - Critical for platform credibility and cultural respect  
**Created:** 2025-08-22  
**Discovery Owner:** Cultural Heritage Team  
**Target Completion:** 3-4 weeks for comprehensive community validation

## 🔍 Community Problem Investigation

### Observed Community Need:
> **"Community members and diaspora users need confidence that Nos Ilha platform content accurately represents authentic Brava Island culture and history, preventing cultural misrepresentation and ensuring respectful preservation of Cape Verdean heritage."**

### Problem Evidence:
- **Content Audit:** 19 main pages analyzed, revealing gaps in historical verification and cultural authenticity
- **Community Concerns:** Need for fact-checking all dates, events, and biographical information
- **Cultural Sensitivity:** Risk of perpetuating inaccurate information about Cape Verdean heritage
- **Platform Credibility:** Essential for establishing trust with both local and diaspora communities

### User Groups Affected:
- **Primary:** Cape Verdean diaspora seeking authentic connection to cultural heritage
- **Secondary:** Cultural researchers, historians, and heritage preservation organizations
- **Stakeholders:** Local Brava Island community, cultural experts, tourism development partners

---

## 🎯 Potential User Outcome

### Hypothesized Outcome:
> **"If we implement comprehensive cultural content verification, community members will trust Nos Ilha as an authoritative source for Brava Island heritage, leading to increased community engagement, accurate cultural preservation, and respectful tourism development."**

### Success Indicators (Hypotheses):
- **Community Trust:** Local and diaspora communities actively refer others to platform for cultural information
- **Content Accuracy:** All historical facts, cultural practices, and geographic information verified by multiple sources
- **Community Engagement:** Increased community contributions and corrections to platform content
- **Cultural Authenticity:** Heritage experts and cultural organizations endorse platform as accurate resource

## 📝 Discovery Research Plan

### Research Questions:
1. **Content Accuracy:** Which historical facts, dates, and biographical information need verification?
2. **Cultural Authenticity:** What cultural practices, traditions, and descriptions require community validation?
3. **Community Authority:** Who are the recognized cultural authorities for different aspects of Brava heritage?
4. **Verification Process:** What validation workflow would community stakeholders trust and support?
5. **Ongoing Maintenance:** How can community validation be sustained as platform content grows?

### Research Methods:
- [ ] **Content Audit Analysis:** Systematic review of all 19 platform pages for verification needs
  - Target: Complete analysis of homepage, about, history, people, towns, music, and contact pages
  - Focus: Identify specific claims requiring fact-checking and community validation
  - Timeline: Week 1 - Complete comprehensive content audit

- [ ] **Community Authority Mapping:** Identify cultural experts and community validators
  - Participants: Local community leaders, diaspora organization heads, academic researchers
  - Format: Interviews to identify recognized authorities for different cultural domains
  - Timeline: Week 1-2 - Map community validation network

- [ ] **Historical Fact-Checking:** Verify dates, events, and biographical information
  - Sources: Cape Verde National Archives, academic publications, primary source documents
  - Focus: Historical timeline, notable figures, cultural development, independence history
  - Timeline: Week 2-3 - Research and verify historical claims

- [ ] **Cultural Practice Validation:** Confirm traditions, customs, and cultural descriptions
  - Experts: Cultural practitioners, elders, heritage preservation organizations
  - Method: Community consultations, cultural expert interviews, diaspora feedback
  - Timeline: Week 3-4 - Validate cultural representations

### Discovery Timeline:
- **Week 1:** Content audit and community authority mapping
- **Week 2:** Historical research and fact-checking initiation
- **Week 3:** Cultural practice validation and community consultation
- **Week 4:** Validation process design and community approval

## 🚀 Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### Criteria for Moving to Implementation:
- [ ] **Community Authority Network:** Clear identification of cultural validators and their domains
- [ ] **Validation Process:** Community-approved process for ongoing content verification
- [ ] **Content Priorities:** Systematic plan for verifying existing content and validating new additions
- [ ] **Community Buy-in:** Local and diaspora stakeholders support validation approach
- [ ] **Sustainability Plan:** Long-term process for maintaining cultural authenticity

### Risk Assessment:
- **High Risk:** Community disagreement about cultural representations or historical interpretations
- **Medium Risk:** Time-intensive validation process slowing content development
- **Low Risk:** Technical implementation of community feedback and validation systems

### Resource Requirements (if proceeding):

- **Process Development:** Community validation workflow and feedback integration system
