## RESEARCH METHODOLOGY

### Phase 1: Primary Sources
1. **Government Archives**
   - Cape Verde National Archives
   - Massachusetts State Archives
   - Portuguese National Library
   - Guinea-Bissau government records

2. **Institutional Collections**
   - Museums (Maritime, Religious, Cultural)
   - Universities (Cornell, Albany Law, Portuguese institutions)
   - Religious organizations (Church of the Nazarene, Capuchin order)

3. **Digital Repositories**
   - Digital Archives (Massachusetts, Portugal, Cape Verde)
   - Online biographical databases
   - Professional organization websites

### Phase 2: Secondary Sources
1. **News Archives**
   - Brava News
   - Albany newspapers
   - Massachusetts local media
   - Portuguese press archives

2. **Cultural Organizations**
   - Cape Verdean community groups
   - Diaspora cultural centers
   - Music and arts organizations

3. **Academic Sources**
   - University collections
   - Research publications
   - Biographical dictionaries

### Phase 3: Creative Solutions
1. **Period Photography**
   - Historical context images from the same era
   - Similar figures from the same profession/region
   - Representative imagery of activities/movements

2. **Artistic Interpretations**
   - Historical illustrations
   - Cultural artistic representations
   - Period-appropriate generic imagery

## TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS

### Image Specifications
- **Format**: JPG preferred for photographs
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1200px width for high-quality display
- **Aspect Ratio**: Flexible, but portrait orientation preferred for individuals
- **File Size**: Optimized for web (under 500KB after compression)

### Legal Considerations
- **Licensing**: Public domain, Creative Commons, or appropriate usage rights
- **Attribution**: Proper credit to sources and copyright holders
- **Permission**: Written permission for copyrighted materials when required

### Backup Strategy
- **Alternative Sources**: Multiple research paths for each image
- **Fallback Options**: Period-appropriate contextual imagery if specific portraits unavailable
- **Placeholder Strategy**: Temporary generic imagery during research phase
